Isabel Group

A visual brand alignment for all business units within Belgium’s leading fintech company.

Dot your i’s.
Find out how I took a lean approach to the alignment of a complete brand family.

Isabel Group is a Belgian company founded in 1995 and has evolved into the country’s biggest fintech. The group combines several strong fintech products. But almost every product, brand or business unit has its own branding and way of communicating, making it hard to project a unified and strong image.

I was asked to come up with an approach for a pragmatic brand alignment. Using strong elements from current visual identities I developed a coherent and flexible design system, allowing all brands from the Isabel group to communicate in a similar fashion.

Well begun is half done.
Defining the brand strategy

During this first step I organized a brand architecture and brand affinity workshop. The purpose was to get a complete and detailed view of all the business units within the Isabel Group and their relation to the mother brand. Together with all the important stakeholders from the group I set out the roadmap, identified every possible pitfall and defined the next steps in the rebranding.
