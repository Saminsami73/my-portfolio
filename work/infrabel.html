Infrabel

Designing the new public website for the Belgian Railway Infrastructure Manager.

All aboard!
Have a closer look on how I redesigned this comprehensive platform.

Infrabel manages the entire Belgian railway infrastructure. It was founded in 2005 by the NMBS and sails it's own course since the liberalisation of the railway network.

I was commissioned for a complete redesign of Infrabel’s public website.

What I did

UX design | UI design | Web Developed by coding

Getting up to speed.
Discovery & content inventory workshops

In a series of co-creative workshops, I focused on the needs of both Infrabel and their target audiences. I mapped the top tasks and defined the key messages. Once this was done, I developed out page models to experiment with different concepts.

Setting up the track.
Wireframing

After the page models I moved on to more detailed wireframes, highlighting the new storyline and all the functionality.

Slanted & blue, we love you.
A fresh design style for Infrabel’s new user interface
