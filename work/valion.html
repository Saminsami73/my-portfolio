Valion

A fresh new brand and website for a promising data consulting company.

To infinity and beyond!
Discover how I helped with the launch of a new brand and website.

Valion started their journey in 2016. Back then, they were known as Smartie. But as you can imagine, it’s hard to be taken seriously when you share your company name with a famous tube of coloured candy.

So it was time for a new name and a matching visual identity. And that’s where I came in. In just a few weeks time, I found their new name, matched it with a colourful visual identity and developed their public website.

What I did

Naming | Visual identity | Web design | Development

When vision brings value.
Coming up with a new name.

The first step of this project was a co-creative naming workshop. I defined the company’s values and mapped differentiators and criteria for their new name. After an intensive brainstorming session I came up with Valion. A strong name with an international appeal. Not only is it a combination of Value and Vision but it also means champion in Finnish.

A bright new future.
‍Visual identity

A new name needs a matching logo. I came up an organic and fresh brand mark and matching design system. By combining the letter ‘V’ with the infinite symbol I highlighted the company’s successful end-to-end strategy.

Bold blobs & bouncy buttons.
UX & UI design

The website is where their new story and visual identity come together. I designed a fresh and dynamic interface and used Webflow to bring the entire experience to life.
